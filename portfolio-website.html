About Me

I am a conceptual architect and designer exploring the relationships between form, space, and human experience through various mediums including vase design, drawing, and photography.

My work examines the boundaries between functional objects and artistic expression, seeking to create pieces that challenge conventional perceptions of space and materiality.

With a background in traditional architecture and fine arts, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to each project, drawing inspiration from urban environments, natural forms, and the interplay of light and shadow.
